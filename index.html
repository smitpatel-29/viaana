Who We Are

Worldwide Non-profit
Charity Organization

Viaana Foundation was born from a simple yet profound belief—that every child, especially young girls, deserves the power of education and the promise of a better future.

What began as a mission to uplift children deprived of learning opportunities due to financial and societal barriers has grown into a movement for change.
