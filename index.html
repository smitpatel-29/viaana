Who We Are

Worldwide Non-profit
Charity Organization

Viaana Foundation was born from a simple yet profound belief—that every child, especially young girls, deserves the power of education and the promise of a better future.

What began as a mission to uplift children deprived of learning opportunities due to financial and societal barriers has grown into a movement for change.

What We Do?

We believe that we can save more lives with you

Vocational Training

Vocational training is key to opening doors for employment and personal growth. We offer programs that equip individuals with the skills they need for a successful career.

Women Empowerment

We empower women by providing the tools they need to succeed—whether through skills training, financial independence programs, or leadership development.

Healthcare

Access to quality healthcare is a basic right for all. Viaana Foundation works to provide essential health services, from medical camps to child healthcare initiatives.

Education

We are committed to empowering children with the gift of education. Through scholarships and community learning, we break the cycle of poverty and foster growth.

Environment

Sustainability is at our heart. We focus on conservation through tree plantations and waste management to protect our planet for future generations.

Affordable Housing

We support initiatives to provide affordable housing. partnering with communities to ensure every family has a safe, secure place to call home.
